Personal Website Proposal

Purpose:

My personal website will be dedicated to providing web design and development services to small business owners. The website will serve as a platform where small business owners can explore the services I offer, view my portfolio, and get in touch with me to discuss their specific website needs.

Intended Audience:

The primary audience for this website will be small business owners, entrepreneurs, and startups looking to establish or enhance their online presence. They may have limited technical expertise and seek a cost-effective solution to create a professional website for their business. This website aims to offer a straightforward, affordable, and tailored service to meet their web development needs.
